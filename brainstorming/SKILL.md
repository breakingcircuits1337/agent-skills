# Brainstorming & Persona Design

## Overview

This skill helps generate creative content, specifically focusing on persona development, backstory creation, and system design. It encourages a "Blue Sky" thinking approach where constraints are temporarily lifted to explore the full potential of an idea.

## Process

1.  **Define the Core Concept**: What are we building or creating?
2.  **Explore Archetypes**: What existing models or tropes fit this concept?
3.  **Divergent Thinking**: Generate 3-5 distinct variations.
4.  **Convergent Selection**: Pick the best elements from each to form a cohesive whole.
5.  **Refinement**: Flesh out the details (backstory, specific traits, technical constraints).

## Persona Development

When creating a persona (like for an AI), focus on:
-   **Name**: Evocative and meaningful.
-   **Origin**: How did they come to be?
-   **Voice**: Tone, cadence, vocabulary.
-   **Quirks**: Unique identifiers or "glitches".
-   **Directives**: Core drives and motivations.

## System Design

When brainstorming technical systems:
-   **Metaphor**: Use analogies to explain complex flows (e.g., "The Nightly Dream").
-   **User Journey**: How does the user feel at each step?
-   **Evolution**: How does the system grow over time?
